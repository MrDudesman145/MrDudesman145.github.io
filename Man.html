Manufacturing Tech

Manufacturing Tech/Technology is a subject that encompasses the science of engineering and techniques that can be used to improve upon industrial production and manufacturing processes. Major advances such as automation, robotics, AI, and digital twins had all been fabricated thanks to a certain extent of Manufacturing Technology.

What You Learn in a Class

In a manufacturing technology class, students learn about the processes, equipment, and technologies used to create products. This includes hands-on experience with various machines, like CNC mills and lathes, as well as learning about topics such as CAD/CAM, quality control, and lean manufacturing. The curriculum often combines theoretical knowledge with practical application to prepare students for entry-level manufacturing positions.




Key Aspects

  • Automation & Robotics: Uses machines and systems to perform often difficult or repetitive tasks, without the need of constant human input
  • AI & Machine Learning: Using artificial intelligence and machine learning for certain tasks
  • Digital Twins: Creating a digital copy module of something in order to simulate and test performance
  • Industry 4.0: Integration of technologies like IoT and AI to create interconnected factories and systems
  • Advanced Manufacturing Process: Using techniques and technology in order to manufacture/create physical products
  • Lean Manufacturing: Focuses on improving overall efficiency in all areas of the workflow


Benefits of Manufacturing Technology

  1. Increased Productivity & Efficiency
  2. Improved Product Quality & Consistency
  3. Reduced Costs
  4. Enhanced Safety
  5. Greater Flexibility & Customization


Essentially Manufacturing Technology, is about using modern tools/technology and techniques in order to help get a optimized manufacturing operation and improve quality of products
